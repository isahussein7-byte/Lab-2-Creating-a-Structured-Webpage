Classic Guacamole

Student: Isa Hussein CPRG213 Date: January 27, 2026

Recipe Overview

This guacamole is fresh, salty, and straightforward: ripe avocados, crisp onion, tomato, jalapeño, and (optional) cilantro. It’s quick to make and works as a dip, taco topping, or side for any Mexican-style meal.

Photo

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe Hass avocados, halved
  • 1/4 cup red or yellow onion, finely diced
  • 2 plum tomatoes, seeded and finely diced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and finely diced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Remove the pit from the avocado halves. Using a tablespoon, scoop out the avocados into a small mixing bowl, then gently mash with a fork.
  2. Add the tomatoes, onion, jalapeños, and cilantro (if using). Mix well.
  3. Add the salt and cayenne pepper. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve.
